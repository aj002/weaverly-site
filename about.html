Welcome to Weaverly.

We believe every woman deserves a space to be seen, heard, and deeply understood. Whether you're entering a new phase of life, building community in a new city, or simply seeking meaningful conversation, Weaverly is a place where real connection begins.

Our gatherings are thoughtfully curated to move beyond small talk. With guided dialogue, shared experiences, and intentional design, we create spaces that feel both grounding and expansive—spaces where belonging comes naturally.

We’re Ananya and Aastha, lifelong friends who created Weaverly out of a shared belief: that friendship in adulthood should feel as effortless and authentic as it did when we were young. We know the joy of being truly known—and we want to help you find that too.

Come as you are. Stay for the stories, the laughter, the tears—and leave with something lasting.
